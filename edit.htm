    About Me:

    Click to edit text

    From Data to Dreams: Shahsawar's Journey a 22-year-old dream weaver hailing from a quaint village in the heart of Punjab, Pakistan. With a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and with limited financial resources, my journey into the world of data was far from easy. My journey into the captivating realm of data science has been anything but conventional. But my passion for computers and the desire to make a mark in the data science field were unwavering. I had a deep fascination for math, and I always believed that data held the power to transform lives. It could be the key to making the world a better place.

    However, being non-technical and from a humble background, the path ahead was hazy, and I often felt like my dreams would remain just that - dreams.

    Then, a ray of hope emerged when Dr. Irfan Malik, the founder of Xeven Solutions in Lahore, Pakistan, showed our youth the path forward. He opened the doors to the world of data science and machine learning without any fees or charges. That's when my journey began I embarked on this journey with the first batch of students in Pakistan, dedicating four months to studying the basics. The experience was transformative. It illuminated a new world in my mind, where data had the power to impact lives positively.

    Today, I walk on a path similar to my Mentor, Dr. Irfan Malik. Every day, I move closer to my goal, learning and growing along the way. My dream is to be so successful that I can inspire other young minds to dream big - to show them that they too can achieve their aspirations.

    I believe in utilizing my skills and talents to make a difference in the lives of others. The world of data science is a place where data can change lives, and I'm witnessing it firsthand. I'm committed to continuing on this path, and I invite you to join me. This journey has only just begun."
